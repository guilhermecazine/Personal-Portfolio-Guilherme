About me 😃

Hi, my name is Guilherme. I'm 23 years old and I was born and raised in Brazil 🇧🇷. At the age of 7 I moved to England 🏴󠁧󠁢󠁥󠁮󠁧󠁿 with my parents and returned to Brazil at the age of 9. Since then, I've always wanted to return here and I managed to make that dream come true in 2020. At the time I was studying Civil Engineering, but I decided to leave university to follow my dream of living in England.

When I went to Brazil to visit my family and friends, I began to experience a dilemma. I really wanted to return to my home, but at the same time, I didn't want to give up on my dream of living abroad, so I started looking for a way out. After researching the different areas in which I could work from home 🧑🏻‍💻, the one that caught my attention the most was Web and Software development and so I started to embark on this new journey.

I am currently studying Full Stack Web Development with Altcademy and I intend, in the future, to start a university course in Computer Science. 👨🏻‍🎓

This website is a showcase of all the projects I have developed so far. I hope to have this portfolio full of incredible things from now on and be able to embark on this new journey. 🎉
